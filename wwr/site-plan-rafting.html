Overview

Purpose

To give people access and information about the prices, requirements and general info about water rafting.You can also make reservations to make the best out of your experience.

Audience

Who are the targeted customers? A page for adventurous people. Whether they are, young old, experienced or is their first time. All they need is the motivation to do it! What do they want? Want to go on a solo, with friends or family adventure. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They can find the option that best suits their needs. If they want to go solo, with friends or family, to know the prices and what can adapt better to them.
